| B1 | `appendToDraft` / `findActiveDraft` is a non-transactional read-modify-write with no optimistic lock — `lib/repeat.ts:305-318` does `findActiveDraft` → `parseCart` → `cart.lines = [...cart.lines, ...newLines]` → `saveDraft` (which just `update`s the whole cart column), and the bulk route's `findActiveDraft` skip-check (`app/api/admin/repeat/bulk/route.ts:53-68`) is not in the same transaction either. This is the core data path for both customer repeat and staff repeat. | `lib/repeat.ts:305-318`, `lib/order-builder/draft-store.ts:56-59`, `app/api/admin/repeat/bulk/route.ts:53-68` | sec M-1, sec M-2, quality H1 |
