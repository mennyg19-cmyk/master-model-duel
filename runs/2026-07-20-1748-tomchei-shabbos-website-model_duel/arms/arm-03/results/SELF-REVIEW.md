| SR-B1 | blocker | `lib/payments/post-payment.ts` (`beginStaffRefund` / `resolveStaffRefund`); `app/api/admin/orders/[id]/refund/route.ts`; `app/api/webhooks/stripe/route.ts` (`handleRefund` / `handleChargeRefunded`) | Staff refund is DB-first: a placeholder row is committed under `stripeRefundId = pending_${idempotencyKey}` before Stripe is called.
